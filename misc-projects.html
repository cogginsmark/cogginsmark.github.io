Miscellaneous Projects

The connections I’ve built through my work with online personalities have led me to come into contact with many talented people. We would discuss potential projects and great ideas --- although not all come to fruition. Here are some of the projects I’ve worked on.

Kimcella Bot (2019-Present) - a Discord bot for the Twitch livestreamer Kimcella. This bot is used to moderate her Discord server, primarily for announcing when the streamer goes live. It is in active development and more features are to come.

Mixer Expansion Pack (2019) - a web browser extension for Mixer.com emulating the Twitch.tv equivalent Better Twitch TV, in which streamers can enable custom emotes in their chat that their viewers can use. The benefit of custom emotes extend from expressing better emotion, to creating a community in which its foundation is its emotes. MEP also allowed user to choose a Dark and Black theme for Mixer, affecting every element on the site. The project reached final stages but publishing issues resulted in its incompletion.

Scuffed.com (2019) - a platform oriented towards in-real-life (IRL) streaming. I was approached about designing the chat icons and badges that would be used on the site. The following was my concept for the site badges. Scuffed.com was eventually scrapped.
