Greekgodx (2018-Present)

Greekgodx is a popular live streamer on the platform Twitch. He is most recognized for his variety gaming content and endearing friendship with the top league player loltyler1.

Dimitri was referred to me by Ice Poseidon, and since then I’ve maintained ongoing contact maintaining a prominent role in his community. Greek’s community outside of Twitch resides on his reddit page, /r/greekgodx. In 2019, I redesigned his subreddit to a accomodate both versions of the site: old.reddit.com and reddit.com. Old reddit relies on css, while new reddit has its own community customization tools. It was an interesting challenge to translate a single design into both of these formats. The result is a consistent look, perfect for a consistent streamer.
