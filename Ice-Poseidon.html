Ice Poseidon (2017-Present)

I do commission designs for the streamer Ice Poseidon (ice_poseidon), primarily for promotion or marketing of his livestreams. I came into contact with Ice's manager, @JacobK_Cx, after submitting a poster that Ice loved and wanted to use. Ice soon became my biggest client. The following is some of the work I've done the past three years.

In 2020 I was tasked with rebranding Ice Poseidon to reflect his shift in content.
To accomplish this, I opted to use a rounded typeface accompanied with a new, equally friendly trident emblem. The new bubbly appearance is reminiscent of his previous branding, but modernized and fits right in line with his more positive approach to live streaming to the masses.

Social Media Banners

Throughout his career, Ice Poseidon has made headlines for his over-the-top and highly produced live streaming events. To advertise the events to new viewers, I was often requested to design a poster that would be advertised on his social media or used as a livestream thumbnail. Usually, these were requested with only the name and time/location of the event provided, so I was free to express my creativity and imagination.
